# Future You

Mission

Help people enjoy their money today without screwing over tomorrow.

Core Philosophy

1. Bills
2. Savings
3. Spending

Savings are not leftovers.
Savings are part of the plan.

Future You is not a budgeting app.

Future You is a financial planning and decision-making app.

Core Questions

- Am I okay?
- Can I afford this?
- What should I do next?

Primary Features

- Safe To Spend
- Rent Protection
- Financial Health
- Future Warnings
- Goal Visualization
- Household Budgets
- Business Budgets
- What If Simulator

Success Metric

A user should be able to open the app and understand their financial situation in less than 30 seconds.
